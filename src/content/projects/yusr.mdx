## Overview

Many people with mobility limitations struggle to confirm whether a place is truly accessible before visiting. Accessibility details are often missing, outdated, or spread across different sources—making simple plans stressful and sometimes impossible.

YUSR solves this by providing an interactive map that helps users find accessible places and understand accessibility information clearly, so they can plan with confidence.

## Problem

Reliable accessibility information is hard to find. This leads to frustration, wasted trips, and limits people’s ability to participate in everyday activities and community life.

## Solution

YUSR offers a clear, community-powered accessibility platform where users can:

- **View** detailed accessibility information for each place
- **Search & filter** locations by accessibility features
- **Submit** evaluations using standardized criteria
- **Share** feedback to improve data accuracy over time
